Common Roofing Problems and Solutions

Roofs, like all parts of a house, are subject to wear and tear. Here are some common problems that can arise:

Missing Shingles

Missing shingles compromise your roof’s integrity, leaving the underlayment exposed to the elements, meaning the wood of your attic is subject to the outside weather. This exposure can lead to water damage, leaks, and increased energy costs due to poor insulation. Florin Roofing Company offers prompt shingle replacement using high-quality materials that match your existing roof, ensuring seamless protection and preserving your home’s appearance. We’ll ensure the shingles match and are properly affixed to the roof again to shield it from future weather.

Roof Leaks

Roof leaks can result from damaged flashing, cracked shingles, or general wear and tear. Ignoring leaks can lead to significant structural damage, mold growth, and interior damage. Our expert leak detection and repair services pinpoint the source of the leak and provide effective, long-lasting solutions, preventing further damage to your home and ensuring a dry, safe living environment. We’re skilled at finding and repairing those subtle leaks that can cause the most damage over time.

Sagging Roof

A sagging roof is a serious issue indicating structural problems, such as water damage, rot, or inadequate support. This not only poses a safety risk but also diminishes your home’s value. Florin Roofing Company conducts thorough roof inspections to identify the underlying causes of sagging and provides comprehensive repair or replacement services to reinforce your roof and restore its structural integrity, ensuring the safety and stability of your home.

Granule Loss

Granule loss from asphalt shingles is a natural part of the aging process, but excessive loss can indicate that your roof is nearing the end of its lifespan. Without the protective granules, shingles become vulnerable to UV damage and water penetration. We offer roof replacement services with high-quality materials that are resistant to granule loss, extending the life of your roof and protecting your home from the elements.

Algae Growth

Algae growth on your roof not only looks unsightly but can also trap moisture, leading to shingle deterioration. We offer roof cleaning services using safe, eco-friendly methods to remove algae and prevent future growth. This improves your home’s curb appeal and prolongs the life of your roof.

Standing Water on Flat Roofs

Standing water on flat roofs, also known as ponding, can cause significant damage over time. The constant moisture exposure can lead to leaks and structural weakening. Our flat roof repair services address the underlying causes of ponding, such as improper drainage or sagging, and provide solutions to ensure proper water runoff and prevent future problems. Keeping your flat roof dry is our expertise

Granules in Gutters

Excessive granules in your gutters are a sign that your asphalt shingles are deteriorating. As shingles age, they lose their protective granules, which end up in your gutters. Sagging Ceilings

Increased Energy Bills

An unexpected increase in your energy bills can be a sign that your roof is not properly insulated.
